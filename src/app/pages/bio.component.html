Stephen Fluin

Stephen is VP of Product for HeroDevs. Stephen is a former Angular core team member and current Angular GDE. Stephen builds Product and DevRel teams that solve real world problems faced by developers and businesses, and advocates for the needs of users and the community.

With ~25 years in the Software Development industry, Stephen has served as an executive technologist, consultant, and entrepreneur at Google, Trello, Chainlink Labs, and more.

Stephen applies deep technical knowledge and business acumen to accelerate software development. As an avid fan of wearables and the decentralized internet, he frequently collaborates with businesses and developers in the community.
